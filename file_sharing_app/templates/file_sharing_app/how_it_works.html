How QuickShare Works

Simple, secure, and ephemeral file sharing in three easy steps.

1

Upload Your File

Select any document, image, or file from your device. As soon as you hit upload, your file is securely transferred to our isolated server environment. No accounts or logins are required.

2

Share the Secure Link

We instantly generate a unique, cryptographically secure URL link. Copy this link and send it to your intended recipient via email, Slack, or your preferred messaging app.

3

Burn After Reading

The moment the recipient clicks "Download Now", the file is transferred to them and immediately, permanently deleted from our server. If they do not download it within 24 hours, it is automatically wiped anyway to ensure your data never lingers online.
